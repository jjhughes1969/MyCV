CWC Addict

A watch collector for many years, a few years back I started a website and blog to document the history of CWC, a watch company started in London in 1972 to supply watches to the British military.

CWC Addict is the only resource of its kind for collectors of these watches, and publishes an (almost) definitive guide to each type of watch the company has produced, as well as occasional research in to the history of the company and military watches in general.

I have a website cwcaddict.com and also an Instagram account @cwc_addict.
